About Us

Here at Gotham Gym we put your goals to be a leaner, stronger, more confident you first. We have a huge weight room with every type of state-of-the-art machine known to be invented for any gym in the world. Our cardio machines can accomodate any customer, from marathon runners to those who are just trying to stay healthy. After your workout, feel free to jump into one of our ice baths or relax your muscles in our steam room or sauna.

If you come in with a goal but you are having a difficult time figuring out where to start, don't hesitate to get with one of our world class, friendly personal trainers. There is always a few ready to help and offer assistance with tips to get you started. Sign up for your first free training session or maybe a monthly plan!
